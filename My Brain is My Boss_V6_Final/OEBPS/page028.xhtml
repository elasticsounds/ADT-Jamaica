GLOSSARY OF TERMS

Word

Definition

Audio

1. Arteries

Blood vessels inside your body that carry blood from the heart to other parts of the body.

2. Body

The body is made up of different parts. Some parts are inside and some parts are outside.

3. Bones

Bones form the skeleton of your body. They help to support and protect your body.

4. Boss

The one who is in charge. Someone who tells you what to do and how to do it.

5. Brain

An organ inside our head that controls everything that we do.

6. Chest

The top part of the front of the body from your neck to your waist.

7. Eyes

The part of the body that you use to see.

8. Feet

The parts of the body that you use for walking.
